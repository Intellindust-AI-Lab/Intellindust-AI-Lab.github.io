About Us

Intellindust AI Lab, is dedicated to advancing computer vision and artificial intelligence for edge devices. With expertise in imaging systems, efficient deep learning, and multimodal AI, our mission is to create high-performance, reliable, and efficient AI solutions for real-world applications.

Research Directions

  • Vision-Language Models for Edge Devices
  • Lightweight Real-Time Object Detection
  • Multimodal Sensor Fusion and Embodied Perception
  • RAW-Domain Video Enhancement and Detection
  • Trustworthy and Interpretable AI

We are actively looking for talented interns and researchers. If you are interested, please contact us at shenxi@intellindust.com.
